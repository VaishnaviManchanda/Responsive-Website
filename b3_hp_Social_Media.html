Analyzing Social Media: Future Scope

Social media is becoming an advertising media for all the businesses worldwide. The tremendous amount of data collection and insights generation which is done by them allowing them to run ads in the community of respective interested people of that community. However not all the social media platforms are going survive in the long run. At first people started using Orkut as it was the first online social media app in early 20’s but then continuous innovation and popularity of Facebook disrupted Orkut completely. And many social media came after that with disruptive and innovative business model such as Instagram, whatsapp and Linkedin.

New generation people are turning up their focus more on Instagram as compared to Facebook. And this is because relatively simplicity of user options provided in application as well as better recommendation model as compared to Facebook. The top metric that Instagram has over Facebook is engagement. A social media engagement study reported that brands see a median engagement rate of 0.09% per Facebook post, in comparison to a 1.60% median engagement rate per post on Instagram. After Instagram, Linkedin is showing positive market trends because of it’s continues launches of new features such as sales navigator and premium business, etc.

Future market scope and trends:

1. Data segmentation

The techniques of data segmentation will improve at large scale making more and more businesses to identify their repetitive visitors and their data more clearly. It will enhance business profits as well as it will provide better matching with customers need.

2. Renovation in data management and safeguarding

Social media now days are having more concerned regarding security and privacy. Especially, after data breach happened at Facebook the concerned regarding this are highly serious. However, more better encryption services and authenticated product will serve community in better way in future.

3. Sentimental Analysis

Machine learning based and lexicon based approaches are widely used for sentiment analysis by computer scientist and engineers. Few research studies have combined these two approaches to get relatively beer performance. The machine learning approach uses diﬀerent classiﬁcation techniques like Naive Bayes, Maximum Entropy and Support Vector Machine for classifying text. The Lexicon based approach uses a sentiment dictionary with opinion words and match them with the data to determine polarity whether it is positive or negative. The hybrid approach combines the machine learning and lexicon based techniques to overcome their individual drawbacks and beneﬁt from each other's merit

4. Video content will be the primary content

Video has become a major trend and types of content people prefer to consume. In fact, it is what makes YouTube the second largest search engine in the world. They are also the much-loved type of content for marketers because they can potentially give a huge return of investments (ROI). The major platform like Facebook and Instagram are pushing hard for videos. Instagram’s recent algorithm gives more preferences to videos when they are ranking for the content. In fact, a study by Cisco stated that by 2020, 75% of the mobile traffic will be video. It all indicates that Video Marketing is the future.

5. Web Scrapping

TWeb scrapping is majorly used extract large amount of data from websites. Now, as social media is becoming more engaging and collecting more and more data of their users and gaining insights. Thus, companies will be using web scrapping at large scale to know about what their user exactly thinking and talking about their brand. They can also extract detailed data regarding what people think about their competitors. This all will revolutionize experience of user as well as for businesses.

6. Heavy use of AR

AR can really be a disruptive technology for many businesses if implemented at advance level. Snapchat is already focusing on AR innovation which might be game changing for social media industry. Various functions of AI can be implemented such as enhancing brand and buying experience as well as live events and videos can also enhance user experience and engagement.
